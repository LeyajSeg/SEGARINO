The Rumbling
The Rumbling (地鳴らし Jinarashi?) was a cataclysmic event involving Karl Fritz's Wall Titans marching across the earth, destroying all life upon it. For over a century, it was a key deterrence factor for the Subjects of Ymir on Paradis Island until the year 854 when Eren Yeager unhardened the Walls, freed the Wall Titans, and began the Rumbling.

Miyamoto Musashi
Vagabond (宮みや本もと武む蔵さし, Miyamoto Musashi) is one of the main protagonists in Vagabond, and a historical figure considered one of the greatest swordsmen in Japanese history. As a young man he embarks on a journey to become the strongest samurai after being exiled from his village.
